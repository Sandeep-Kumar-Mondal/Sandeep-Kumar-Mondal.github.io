Research Areas

Multi-wavelength Data Analysis: Gamma-ray, X-ray, and optical/UV variability studies.

Spectral Energy Distribution Modelling: Leptonic modeling using JetSeT, GAMERA, Naima.

Neutrino–Gamma-ray Connection: Exploring high-energy neutrino associations.

Quasi-Periodic Oscillations: Time-series analysis of AGN variability.

Cosmic Ray Simulations: UHECR propagation and cascade simulations.
