Multi-wavelength Study of Mrk 180

This work presents a comprehensive analysis of the high-frequency peaked BL Lac object Mrk 180 using long-term X-ray and gamma-ray observations.

Multi-wavelength SED fit for Mrk 180.

The study focuses on:

  • Spectral variability across energy bands
  • Correlation between X-ray and gamma-ray emission
  • Synchrotron and inverse Compton processes
  • Constraints on jet physical conditions
