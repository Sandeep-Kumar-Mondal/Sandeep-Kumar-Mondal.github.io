I am an astrophysicist studying high-energy phenomena in the universe, including blazars, gamma-ray emission, and multiwavelength spectral energy distribution modeling. My research involves analysis of data from missions such as Fermi-LAT and Swift, along with theoretical modeling of relativistic jets and particle acceleration processes.
